More information about Emma Watson

Actress

Emma Watson is a British Actress. She is famous since 2001 by playing in the famous movie Harry Potter ! She played the iconic character of Hermione Granger.

Fashion and modeling

Having an interest in fashion, she appeared in Teen Vogue magazine at age of 15 and released a clothing line in 2010 in collaboration with Safia Minney for her brand named People Tree.

Ambassador for the United Nations

she was named a goodwill ambassador by UN Women in 2014 and she actively fights for gender equality.
